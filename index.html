Eisenhower Method

The Eisenhower Method helps you prioritize tasks by their urgency and importance. Tasks that are both urgent and important should be completed first. Important but not urgent should be scheduled to complete at a later time. Urgent but not important should be prioritized last or delegated. Neither urgent or important should be deleted from your to-do list. Practice prioritizing tasks using the matrix below.
